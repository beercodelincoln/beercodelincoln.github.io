Beer and Code Lincoln, NE

Socialize, write code, and drink good beer!

Take your passion for programming and love of beer and join us for our monthly Beer and Code meetup.

This is a time to work on projects you enjoy, meet other developers, share tricks of the trade, and enjoy some good beer.

Don't have a side-project to work on? No worries, come as you are and we can either help you think of something or maybe even come up with a joint project.

Also, don't forget to bring your laptop!
